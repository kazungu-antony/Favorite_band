Sauti-Sol

Sauti Sol is one of the famous bands in kenya. The group name translates toVoice in the Sunand it was founded in 2005 by one of the band members. The band has four members; Bien-Aime Barasa, Willis Chimano, Savara Mudigi and Polycarp Otieno. Apart from Otieno, the others have been together for 15 years and have also gone to the same High school.Baraza met Otieno at Aliance Francais in Nairobi where he was perfoming and later on joined the team to form the band. The band produces very interesting, catching and unique songs. The taste of tradional music and the Rhumba scensation in their music always makes me want to hear their songs more and more. Their facinating beats and grooves always makes one want to dance.
